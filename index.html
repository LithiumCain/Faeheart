Book One · Faeheart Chronicles

Of Stormsalt
& Smoke

OS&S · Drafting 2026

A fae girl who never believed in destiny bonds. A storm-born wolf raised in silence and secrecy. One encounter during the Faerie Parade that neither of them can forget — and a bond older than the realm itself, stirring awake.

When Allura Starling arrives at Selenari Nexus University, she finds the wolf with gold-flecked eyes is a boy. When he catches her scent on campus, his wolf nearly tears him apart. Their bond ignites something ancient in the realm — and something the Blight has been quietly waiting for.

"This is not a love story. This is a bond. And it begins with stormsalt and smoke."

The World

Xiphos

A world where magic is fading, dragon eggs lie dormant, wolf shifters are thought to be myths, and belief in ancient bonds has all but vanished — until two students arrive at a hidden university deep in an enchanted forest and change everything.

✦

The Seven Fonts

Auric · Sanguine · Tidal · Ember · Primal · Umbral · Aether. Each student chooses one Font at mid-year. Allura accidentally touched two. Reign received one through a bond that shouldn't exist.

🐺

The Bond System

Ancient. Irreversible. Not a choice. The fae mate-bond awakens through scent, proximity, and magic — and once it ignites, the realm itself responds.

🏛

Selenari Nexus

A hidden magical university grown from living trees and moonstone, deep in the ancient forests of Xiphos. Where fae, wolf-kind, and dragon-bonded study side by side — and where something is spreading beneath the roots.

🌑

The Blight

Magic is fading across the realm. Dragon eggs won't hatch. Bonds go dormant. Something ancient and corrupting moves beneath the surface — and it stirred the moment their bond ignited.

⚔️

The Courts

Fae courts, wolf sovereignty, dragon clans, siren alliances — a political landscape built over millennia of fragile peace. One bond is about to unravel all of it.

📜

The Naming System

Names in Xiphos carry magic. Wolves name themselves with their first howl. Fae names shift with their font awakening. Some names are given to hide what someone truly is — until they're ready to be known.
